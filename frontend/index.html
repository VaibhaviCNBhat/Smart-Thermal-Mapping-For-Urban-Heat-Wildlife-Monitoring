📊 Dataset · Landsat 8

  • Optical Bands: B2, B3, B4 (RGB)
  • Thermal Band: B10 (TIR)
  • DN → Brightness Temperature (K)
  • Cloud mask filtering (<10%)
  • Patch size: 256×256 pixels

⚡ Loss Functions

  • L1 Reconstruction Loss
  • SSIM Structural Loss
  • Thermal Energy Consistency
  • Prevents texture hallucination
  • Physics-aware constraints

  • RMSE: 1.83 K
  • Scale: 4× enhancement ratio
  • 30m → 7.5m effective resolution
